The Kimball Model is referred too as the dimensional model

The Kimball model is based on two key concepts: Facts and Dimensions
1. Facts are metrics from a business process
2. Dimensions are context surrounding a business process

Facts and Dimensions form a star schema


Fact: property sales
Dimensions: Lender, Salesperson, Date, Property, Payment Type


Fact Tables (measures)
1. Measurements or metrics from your business process
2. Keys establish relationships between fact and dimension tables

Dimensions (who, what, when, where, why)

Shared business concepts
1. person, employee, customer, vendor

static or slowly changing data

dimensions contain many columns and few rows
