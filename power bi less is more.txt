three questions to ask before adding anything to a dashboard

1. does this contribute to the story
2. is this the right visual element
3. is this necessary

tailor your story to the clients needs

flexibility limits your control

understand user needs and the types of stories they can pull from the data

line charts are great for showing trends over time

line charts are not good for categorical data

bar charts are better for categorical data

emphasis something using one color

negative space help us understand what is important
